detecting outliers - Mainly The 1.5*IQR Rule 
(Outliers are caculated because - To reduce the risk And to Maximise the profit )

The fences:
Lower =  Q1 - 1.5*IQR
Upper = Q3 - 1.5*IQR

Less than the Lower Fence → Lower outlier
Greater than the Upper Fence → Upper outlier
